Build for Survival

Construct Buildings with Voxels to survive Disasters!
A new Survival game by Typical Developers. Build as efficient as possible to survive a wide range of disasters. You can also improve your chances to live by purchasing more creates to gain ressources you can place to find a safe place. Be prepared against Meteors, Sword Fights, Bomb Rains and much more!
