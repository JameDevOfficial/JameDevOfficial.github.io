Oaklands

Live and free to play!

Oaklands is a free to play Tycoon game. You can chop trees, mine ores, collect items, sell your ressources for money, use your ressources for building, collect limiteds, build systems with logic components and much more!

Build for Survival

Live and free to play!

Build for Survival is a free to play survival game. Play with your friends or alone, doesn't matter. Build clever constructions to survive multiple disasters and earn coins to gain even more ressources!
